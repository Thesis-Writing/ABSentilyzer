Welcome to AB Sentilyzer!

an Aspect-based Sentiment Analyzer tool developed for text analytics with the use of Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing.

AB Sentilyzer is a simple automated tool that allows users to determine the sentiment expressed in an input/s with more granular results. It focuses on Sentiment Analysis and its potential growth in the future as an opportunity to discover more methods to improve the service it provides.


AB Sentilyzer is developed by the students of Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Manila under the Department of Computer Science, 2018-2022.
